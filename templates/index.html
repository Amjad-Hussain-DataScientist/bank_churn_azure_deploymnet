Customer Churn Prediction

Welcome to the Customer Churn Prediction Web Application. This intelligent machine learning system predicts whether a customer is likely to leave a bank based on customer profile and account information. The model has been trained using kaggle data like banking data and helps identify customers who are at risk of churn. Early prediction enables businesses to improve customer satisfaction, reduce churn, and increase long-term customer retention through timely intervention.
